Billing & Insurance

Common questions regarding our pricing, memberships, and payment policies.

Do you accept health insurance?
We are an out-of-network provider. While we do not bill insurance directly, we can provide you with a detailed "superbill" containing appropriate medical coding (CPT codes) for clinical massage that you can submit to your insurance company for potential reimbursement.
Can I pause or cancel my membership?
Yes. Memberships operate on a month-to-month basis after the initial 3-month commitment. You may freeze your account for up to 60 days per calendar year, or cancel with a 30-day written notice. Unused sessions roll over as long as the membership is active.
Do you accept HSA or FSA cards?
Absolutely. Because we offer clinical, therapeutic bodywork, our services generally qualify for HSA (Health Savings Account) and FSA (Flexible Spending Account) spending. We recommend obtaining a letter of medical necessity from your primary care physician to keep on file.
Are gratuities included in the price?
To maintain a purely clinical and medical aesthetic, our establishment operates on a gratuity-free model. Our therapists are compensated at top-tier industry rates. The price you see is the final price you pay, allowing you to focus entirely on your healing.
