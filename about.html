About TherapyNow

Discover the passion, people, and purpose behind our premium bodywork services. We believe that physical recovery leads to ultimate peace of mind.

Our Story

How It All Began.

Founded with a clear vision, TherapyNow was born from a simple realization: everyday professionals and athletes were burning out, living with chronic pain, and lacking a true sanctuary for recovery.

What started as a small boutique therapy space has now grown into a premier clinical bodywork center. We bridge the gap between medical massage and high-end spa relaxation, focusing heavily on targeted pain relief and mobility.

Today, we've helped over 10,000 clients alleviate tension, correct posture, and return to their daily lives feeling completely restored.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To democratize physical wellness by making expert bodywork and clinical massage accessible, personalized, and deeply effective for individuals recovering from injury, chronic stress, or athletic fatigue.

Our Vision

To be the premier therapeutic bodywork destination, recognized for our commitment to holistic healing and transforming the way people prioritize their physical and mental recovery.
